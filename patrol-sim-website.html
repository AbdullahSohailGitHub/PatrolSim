Situation Report

Why Your Last Map Failed

Photogrammetry tiles look impressive from a drone. At street level they collapse — warped geometry, melted intersections, frame drops. The issue wasn't budget. It was the wrong workflow for a training environment.

⚠️

Distorted Photogrammetry

3D tile imports warp and break at street level. Road surfaces become unusable the moment a vehicle tries to follow them.

🚧

Broken Intersections

Melted geometry at junctions makes clean navigation impossible. A training sim demands crisp, drivable corners — not warped tiles.

📉

Frame Rate Collapse

Heavy photogrammetry meshes tank UE5 performance. Consistent frame rates aren't a luxury in a training simulator — they're the baseline.

🔇

Visual Noise Kills Recognition

Overly detailed trees, stores, and facades make landmark recognition harder. Training needs clarity, not detail for its own sake.
